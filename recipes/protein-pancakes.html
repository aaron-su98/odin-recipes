Protein Pancakes

Need a protein boost? Try some delicious homemade protein pancakes!

These fluffy, flavorful pancakes pack a punch of protein for all you gym-goers that need an extra protein kick. Say goodbye to drinking drab, boring protein shakes and say hello to your new favorite protein meal! Add your favorite fruits to send your taste buds on a journey!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground oats
  • 3/4 cup blanched almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/3 cup protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon flax seed meal
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil, or more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons coconut oil, or as needed
  • Optional: 1 cup fresh blueberries, or to taste
