Matcha White Chocolate Chip Cookies

Matcha craving? Look no further with this creation of the best of both worlds! Matcha and cookies!

Who doesn't love matcha and cookies? Take your cravings to the next level with this sweet and earthy delicacy. The appearance of this dessert may be unappealing, but don't be fooled by its guise and experience wondrous balance in flavors so complex that only people that have tried it can understand.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 10 tablespoons green tea powder (matcha)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 320 degrees F (160 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Combine powdered sugar, butter, and brown sugar in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment; beat on medium speed, then increase speed and continue to mix until smooth and creamy.
  3. Mix in 1 1/2 cups plus 2 tablespoons flour, matcha, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until combined.
  4. Mix in egg and vanilla, gradually lowering the speed setting until everything is evenly incorporated and you get a dough-like texture.
  5. Add white chocolate chips and mix in evenly.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set, about 10 minutes.
  8. Cool on the baking sheet briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool, 5 to 10 minutes more.
