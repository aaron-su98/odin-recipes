Protein Pancakes

Need a protein boost? Try some delicious homemade protein pancakes!

These fluffy, flavorful pancakes pack a punch of protein for all you gym-goers that need an extra protein kick. Say goodbye to drinking drab, boring protein shakes and say hello to your new favorite protein meal! Add your favorite fruits to send your taste buds on a journey!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground oats
  • 3/4 cup blanched almond flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/3 cup protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon flax seed meal
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil, or more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons coconut oil, or as needed
  • Optional: 1 cup fresh fruit(s)

Steps

  1. Combine oats, almond flour, coconut flour, protein powder, chia seeds, flax seed meal, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon in a medium bowl.
  2. Combine water, milk, eggs, 1 tablespoon coconut oil, and vanilla in a small bowl.
  3. Mix wet ingredient into dry ingredients.
  4. Heat 1 teaspoon coconut oil in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/3 cup batter into the pan. Spread out with the back of a spoon if needed.
  6. Optional: Add blueberries or desired fruit(s), to taste.
  7. Cook for 2 minutes, flip with spatula, and cook an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Remove pancake to a plate and repeat until all batter is gone.
